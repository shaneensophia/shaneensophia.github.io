You're The Prey

Role : Sound Artist

They call it "the Thing," but that's only because there's no other way to describe it. It looks like a duck, it moves like a duck, but one glance and you know—it’s not a duck. Its beak curls into something that resembles a grin, lined with teeth so sharp they look more like slivers of broken glass. Its eyes… a searing red, hollow, as if peering straight into you, lingering too long.

Everything About Nightmare

Role : Programmer & Sound Artist

Tells the story of a little girl named Alita who was initially sleeping in her room, but in a semi-conscious state, she was suddenly kidnapped by a large and scary monster. Without knowing what happened, Alita suddenly woke up in a dark house, requiring Alita to get out of the house by going through several puzzles and hiding from the monster.

Ashes of Polyhymnia

Role : Programmer & Sound Artist

Tells the story of a group of musicians in the post-modern era who feel that the world seems empty and happiness is hard to attain. This group of musicians shares a common goal: to bring color and joy to the world. However, they face challenges in doing so, as depression has taken over a large part of society. As a result, they travel back in time with a mission to prevent the sadness and emptiness that will engulf the future by performing classic songs.
